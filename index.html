Rules For New Developers

  • 1. Never stop learning.

    Technology advances by leaps and strides ever 3 years. If you stay stuck on only what you know, you will be left behind and your skills and knowledge will become irrelevant. Keep up with current trends in the tech world, but do not try to just onto every single one at the same time.

  • 2. Know more than one language

    A true developer or engineer can pick up just about any language and within a few minutes start writing code. Even if its the first time they have seen that language. Knowing only one language and only being able to program in that language means you are just a programmer or code monkey as they are commonly called.
